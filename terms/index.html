Acceptance and eligibility

By using TalkAir, you agree to these terms and the Privacy Policy. You must be at least 18 years old and legally able to enter an agreement in your jurisdiction. Do not use the service where anonymous voice chat is prohibited.

Acceptable use

You may use TalkAir for lawful personal conversation, language practice, peer study, and skill sharing. You are responsible for what you say, send, and request.

You must not:

  • harass, threaten, stalk, exploit, discriminate against, or impersonate another person;
  • engage in sexual exploitation, grooming, or any sexual interaction involving minors;
  • promote violence, terrorism, self-harm, or unlawful activity;
  • commit fraud, request money deceptively, distribute malware, or seek unauthorized access;
  • share another person's private information without permission;
  • spam, automate abusive connections, overload the service, or bypass safety controls;
  • infringe copyright, trademark, privacy, publicity, or other rights.

Anonymous matches and user content

TalkAir does not verify users' identity, age, location, credentials, qualifications, or claims. Advice from a stranger may be incorrect or harmful. Do not rely on TalkAir conversations as medical, legal, financial, mental-health, emergency, or other professional advice.

You retain responsibility for your speech and text. You grant the service the limited technical permission necessary to transmit content to your matched peer.

Moderation and availability

TalkAir may disconnect sessions, rate-limit traffic, restrict access, or change features to protect users and infrastructure. The current MVP does not promise continuous availability, successful matching, universal network connectivity, preservation of chat, or recovery of interrupted sessions.

Disclaimers and liability

The service is provided on an “as is” and “as available” basis to the extent permitted by law. No warranty is made that users are truthful, calls will connect, content will be safe or accurate, or the service will be error free. Liability limitations and governing-law clauses require completion by qualified counsel for the operator's actual jurisdiction before production use.

Changes and contact

Features and terms may change. Continued use after an updated effective date may constitute acceptance where legally permitted. Before public launch, this section must include the operator's legal name, service address, governing law, dispute process, and contact email.

Frequently asked questions

How old must I be to use TalkAir?

The current MVP is limited to users who are at least 18 years old.

Does TalkAir verify teachers?

No. Teach Mode does not currently verify qualifications or identity.

Can TalkAir ban abusive users?

The service may restrict sessions or access, though the MVP's anonymous design limits account-based enforcement.

Is TalkAir professional advice?

No. Conversations should not be treated as verified medical, legal, financial, emergency, or other professional advice.
