How partner practice can help

The IELTS Speaking test requires you to respond immediately, develop ideas, and remain understandable in a live interaction. Partner practice reproduces the social pressure that solo recording cannot fully provide.

TalkAir is not affiliated with IELTS, the British Council, IDP, or Cambridge University Press & Assessment. A peer cannot provide an official band score, but they can notice hesitation, unclear answers, repetition, and whether you addressed the question.

Run a complete mock speaking session

Part 1: introduction

Spend four to five minutes on familiar questions about home, work, study, interests, or daily life.

Part 2: long turn

Give the candidate one minute to prepare and up to two minutes to speak from a cue-card topic.

Part 3: discussion

Ask broader and more analytical questions related to the Part 2 topic.

Focused review

Discuss fluency, vocabulary variety, grammar range, and understandable pronunciation without inventing an official score.

Sample speaking prompts

Part 1

  • What do you enjoy about the place where you live?
  • How do you normally organize your study time?
  • What kind of technology do you use every day?

Part 2

Describe a skill you would like to learn. Say what the skill is, why you want to learn it, how you would learn it, and explain how it could affect your life.

Part 3

  • Why do adults sometimes stop learning new skills?
  • Should employers pay for professional education?
  • How might artificial intelligence change skill development?

Feedback that supports improvement

Record notes rather than interrupting the mock test. Afterward, identify one strength and two specific improvements. Useful observations include frequent filler words, answers that need examples, repeated basic vocabulary, unclear endings, or a tendency to memorize language that does not fit the question.

Frequently asked questions

Is TalkAir an official IELTS service?

No. TalkAir is an independent conversation platform and is not endorsed by or affiliated with IELTS organizations.

Can a partner give me a band score?

A peer may offer an opinion, but only qualified exam processes produce an official IELTS score.

How do I find an IELTS partner?

Choose Study Mode, select IELTS, and enter matchmaking while another user has selected the same options.

Should I memorize complete answers?

Memorized answers can sound unnatural and may not address the exact question. Practice organizing fresh ideas and supporting them with reasons or examples.
